About Us

Food Manager is a web app that helps food companies manage customers and their food orders for the delivery service. It also helps the company to keep track of customers and the different food items that they can order from the website.


Food Manager has three major functions:

Managing Customer Data: The web app will allow users to add, view, edit, and delete customer data.

Managing Food Data: The web app will allow users to add, view, edit, and delete food data.

Managing Order Data: The web app will allow users to add, view, edit, and delete order data.
